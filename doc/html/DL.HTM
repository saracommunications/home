DL Element

Syntax:

<DL> ... </DL>

Example:


<DL>
<DT>DOG</DT>
<DD> Medium sized furry animal. Often chases small furry animals.</DD>
<DT>CAT</DT>
<DD> Small furry animal. Often chases smaller furry animals.</DD>
<DT>MOUSE</DT>
<DD> Smaller furry animal. Often chased by small furry animals.</DD>
</DL>
DOG.
Medium sized furry animal. Often chases small furry animals.
CAT.
Small furry animal. Often chases smaller furry animals.
MOUSE.
Smaller furry animal. Often chased by small furry animals.
